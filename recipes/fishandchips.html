Tom's Famous Fish and Chips Recipe

This is a recipe made famous as a late night dinner, quick and easy!

Ingreditents:

  • fish
  • chips

Steps:

  1. Microwave the Fish and the Chips. Total time will depend on microwave, 5 minutes for a 1100w inverter microwave when cooking from frozen
  2. Put food onto a sandwich press and press till toasted
  3. Garnish with salt and the juice of a lemon or lemon juice from a bottle
